Shooting Tool

Two tools for the range
Shot Timer
  • Tap Start — after a random 1–4 s delay a loud beep fires as the start signal.
  • Your reaction time is the elapsed time from the beep to your first recorded shot.
  • Follow-up shot times appear as split rows below, each showing total time and the gap since the previous shot.
  • Tap mode — tap anywhere on screen after each shot. Mic mode — the microphone listens for gunshots automatically.
Bullet Drop
  • Select 5.56 or 9mm, your barrel length or platform, and your zero distance.
  • The table shows bullet impact relative to point of aim at each range out to 300 yards.
  • Green = your zero. White = above LoS. Gray / red = below LoS.
